Emily Finch is a candidate for her Masters in Information at the University of Michigan School of Information. In addition she is pursuing a Graduate Certificate in Museum Studies at the University of Michigan Rackham Graduate School. Upon completion of these degrees in 2019 she plans on attending law school and beginning a career in cultural heritage law.

Prior to attending the University of Michigan Emily Finch completed her Bachelors of Arts at Kalamazoo College where she majored in English and History, minored in Political Science and concentrated in American Studies.

For a period of her undergraduate study Emily Finch completed course work at the University of Aberdeen in Aberdeen, Scotland taking courses in contemporary British literature, Spanish politics, and Russian History.

In her free time, Emily enjoys traveling the globe, reading classical literature, baking, visiting museums, and volunteering.
